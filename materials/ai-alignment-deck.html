What is AI Alignment?

The effort to ensure AI systems behave in ways consistent with the goals, values, and intentions of the people they are meant to serve.

CHAOSS AI in Open Source Alignment Working Group · github.com/chaoss/wg-ai-alignment

What is AI Alignment for Open Source?

In the context of open source, we focus on alignment with what we have built, what we know, what we value, and what we expect, in ways that honor the labor and intention of communities.

Potential Layers for AI Alignment

Values

Whose values? Are open source principles being preserved?

Governance

Who decides? Does AI respect how our community makes decisions?

Labor

Whose work? Is contributor labor respected, credited, or extracted?

Legal

Whose rights? Is attribution, licensing, provenance honored?

Knowledge

Who has access? Is knowledge shared openly or enclosed in private systems?

Community

Whose benefit? Does this strengthen the commons or extract from it?
